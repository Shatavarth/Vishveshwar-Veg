About Vishveshwar

Welcome to Vishveshwar Pure Veg & Residency, your perfect retreat nestled in the heart of Shegaon, Maharashtra. As a cherished family-run establishment, we offer a serene haven for pilgrims, travelers, and food enthusiasts visiting this spiritual town.

We are ideally situated to provide convenient access to the revered Shri Gajanan Maharaj Sansthan Temple, offering a peaceful base for your pilgrimage. Beyond the spiritual, adventure and leisure await at nearby attractions such as the thrilling Mauli Water Park, the unique experience of Mauli Sky Dining, and the serene beauty of Anand Sagar Park.
